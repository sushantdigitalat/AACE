What is AACE?

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to the forum of the AAC Institute of India. Our institute is dedicated to serving the manufacturing industry of AAC blocks and panels while providing robust support to the users of AAC blocks on construction sites. We are committed to fostering innovation, sharing knowledge, and driving excellence in the AAC industry. Together, we can build a stronger, more sustainable future for construction.
