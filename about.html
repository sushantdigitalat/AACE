About AACE

Welcome to the AAC Institute of India, the leading platform dedicated to advancing the Autoclaved Aerated Concrete (AAC) block and panel industry. We bring together manufacturers, suppliers, domain experts, and industry professionals to foster collaboration, innovation, and growth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support and enhance the AAC industry by providing resources, facilitating networking opportunities, and promoting best practices. We are committed to driving excellence, sustainability, and technological advancements within the sector.

Our Vision

We envision a thriving AAC industry where innovation and sustainability are at the forefront, contributing to the future of construction. Our goal is to be the premier hub for all AAC-related activities, knowledge sharing, and industry development.
