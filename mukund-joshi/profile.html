About ME

Hello! I am Mukund Joshi, a seasoned concrete technologist and AAC (Autoclaved Aerated Concrete) specialist. My journey in the world of concrete and AAC spans over three decades, during which I have amassed a wealth of knowledge and experience that I am passionate about sharing with others.

Services Offered

As an AAC consultant, Mukund Joshi offers a range of services tailored to meet the needs of manufacturers, builders, and developers:

Technical Consultation

Expert advice on AAC production processes, equipment selection, and quality control.

Project Management

End-to-end support for AAC construction projects, from planning to execution.

Training Programs

Customized training sessions for teams, covering all aspects of AAC technology and application.

Sustainability Consulting

Guidance on incorporating AAC blocks and panels into sustainable building practices.
